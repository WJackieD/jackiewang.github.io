Blue Sun and Black Moon

[Visual] Ding Wang
[Audio] Ding Wang
[Time] June, 2021





+Video Demonstartion

volume
speed

It is a love story of the Blue Sun and the Black Moon, who wander in the endless universe. The hidden poem will emerge gradually if the pressed mouse hovers on them for a few seconds.



+Interaction

Press and move the mouse to wipe away the dust of light to discover the fragments of the poem.



+The Whole Poem

HOW LONELY
HE TRAVELS
IN THIS ENDLESS UNIVERSE

SO SPLENDID
SHE FLARES
WHEN THE MOON OF BLACK MEETS THE SUN OF BLUE

NO STAR SHINES FOR ALL
NO PLANET STILLS TO FALL
LET THE LIGHT ROLL
TO ITS ETERNAL AND SOLE
