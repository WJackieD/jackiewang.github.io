Hue Resonance

[Visual] Ding Wang
[Audio] None
[Time] September, 2020





+Video Demonstartion

volume
speed

The machine wants to draw on its own grid. Once a pair of dots is detected to have the same color, they will be linked with a line of the resonated color. The machine keeps drawing until all the dots are linked, or a stop is called. When the painting is finished, it will get a unique label (a serial number of total framecount and average hue)!



+Interaction

Two dots will link to each other when they accord in color.
