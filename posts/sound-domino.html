Sound Domino

[Visual] Ding Wang
[Audio] Ding Wang
[Time] April, 2021

volume
speed

The sound beeps emerge randomly in the field, and every unit will grow black once an ambient beep is detected. When a unit reaches its maximum, it will start to spread a series of tone waves, like a Sound Domino.



+Interaction

Each unit has a gauge for measuring the accumulated sound energy. The increments depend on the distance to the sound sources.



When the sound energy reaches the maximum, the unit will turn red and trigger a strong tone.



The triggered unit will start a chain of sound reaction by passing the energy to the adjacent units.



The tone waves will keep spreading, but lose some energy at every step.



After triggering, the units will cool down and prepare for a new turn of sound detecting.
